## Getting Started

Empower your users with Mycroft across all platforms. Customize the UI, apply RBAC for secure access, and use tracking dimensions to optimize user experiences, ensuring intuitive and consistent interactions.

## Installation Guide

Mycroft offers two installation options, one designed for open-access websites without user logins, such as landing pages or informational sites, and another for platforms with restricted access for registered users, such as dashboards, customer portals, or internal tools, ensuring you can implement the solution that best suits your website's needs.
