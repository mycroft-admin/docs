## Getting Started

Empower your users with Mycroft across all platforms. Customize the UI, apply RBAC for secure access, and use tracking dimensions to optimize user experiences, ensuring intuitive and consistent interactions.

## Installation Guide

Mycroft offers two installation options: one for websites that don’t require user logins and another for those that do. Choose the approach that aligns with your website’s needs!
